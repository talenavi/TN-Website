Art Print

Vanessa Eryn

This artwork depicts my family trip to bali in one illustration, the main character seen is my little sister (because she's cute). Then she have fun with us and be her light which is the butterfly, along with the ruined background depicts Bali because there's so many traditional monuments and historical buildings like museums. The glowing flower depicts our precious moments with her in Bali, the flowing water represents time, while the water and the glowing flower flows out of the ruined building which is Bali.

The reason why I created this piece is because my little sister recently left and went abroad to study for a long time and she misses the moments we have in Bali, as time flows the moments are washed away with her studies. But even so, we'll never stop guiding her and be her strength and light. Fighting lil sis!

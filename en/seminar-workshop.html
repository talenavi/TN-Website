How Does a Designer Adapt to a New Condition

Wednesday, 27 October 2021
15.00 - 17.00 GMT

How Does a Designer Adapt to a New Condition

Description

These past few years have been the hardest for all of us, including designers. This situation forces us to adapt to this new condition. Therefore, in this seminar we’re going to discuss some tips that designers need to adapt to in this situation, such as this pandemic or in a new environment. We’re also going to talk about the association's experience in adapting to this situation. We’re hoping this seminar could give some insight and new knowledge to the audience whether about the way they adapt to this new condition or working outside their comfort zone.

Saturday, 23 October 2021
10.00 - 12.00 GMT

Typography can Speak Louder than Words

Description

For some people, designers are considered to only revolve around visual aspects. But in reality, designers are more than that. A designer has to be able to hold in many aspects to make a beautiful and proper creation, such as words. Typography is part of a word where it could deliver a message more than the word itself. In this seminar, we’re going to discuss how and why typography could have a stronger meaning than any ordinary word.
